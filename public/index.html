Not sure where to go? Perfect

Manali

Manali is a high-altitude Himalayan resort town in Himachal Pradesh. Set on the Beas River, it’s a gateway for skiing in the Solang Valley and trekking in Parvati Valley.

Shimla

Shimla is the capital of Himachal Pradesh and a popular hill-station among Indian families and honeymooners. Shimla was the summer capital of British India.

Goa

Goa is India's smallest state and unlike any other, known for its endless beaches, stellar nightlife, eclectic seafood, world-heritage listed architecture.

Andaman

Andamans have the most exotic beach and some of them also give the opportunity of trying out a number of water sports such as Scuba Diving, Snorkeling, Sea Walk etc.
